Giovani Gonçalves Ribamar

Doctoral Student in Materials Science

I have under graduated in Metallurgical Engineering by Federal University of Ceará (2015) and have master's degree in Materials Science and Engineering at Federal University of Ceará (2018). Currently, I am Ph.D. candidate in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at University of São Paulo in Quenching and Partitioning in High Carbon Steels field. I have experience in the area of Materials and Metallurgical Engineering, with emphasis on Physical Metallurgy of steels. I also have experience in characterization methods, such as optical and scanning electron microscopy, electron backscatter diffraction, dilatometry, as well as Synchrotron X-ray diffraction.
